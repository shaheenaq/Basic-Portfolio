About Me

While I was born and raised here in the USA, my family roots and traditions are of the Gujarati heritage of Pakistan.

I have a very supportive husband and best friend of 18 years and two beautiful children. Love to hike, read and watch shows such as The Walking Dead and The Flash and Dr. Who.

Traveling and living in a variety of countries has been an on going theme from my childhood with my parents and now as an adult with my own children. Experiencing life and culture in others countries really opens your mind. Some of the places we have lived and visited:

  • Dubai, UAE
  • Berlin, Germany
  • Rome, Italy
  • Cairo, Egypt
  • Sydney, Australia
